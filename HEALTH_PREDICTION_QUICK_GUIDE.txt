🎯 WHAT IT DOES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Predicts risk levels for 4 health conditions:
  • Obesity
  • Diabetes  
  • Heart Disease
  • Nutrient Deficiency

Using Machine Learning based on your profile & lifestyle data.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

📊 INPUT DATA (5 Features)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. AGE (from profile)
   ├─ Example: 35 years
   └─ Normalized: 35/100 = 0.35

2. BMI (calculated from height/weight)
   ├─ Formula: weight(kg) / height(m)²
   ├─ Example: 75kg / 1.7² = 25.95
   └─ Normalized: 25.95/40 = 0.648

3. ACTIVITY LEVEL (from profile)
   ├─ Sedentary (S)    → 0.2
   ├─ Light (L)        → 0.4
   ├─ Moderate (M)     → 0.6
   ├─ Active (A)       → 0.8
   └─ Very Active (V)  → 1.0

4. DIET SCORE (user input - form)
   ├─ Range: 0.0 (poor) to 1.0 (excellent)
   └─ Example: 0.6 = Good diet quality

5. CALORIE RATIO (user input)
   ├─ Formula: calories_consumed / daily_target
   ├─ Example: 2200/2000 = 1.1 (slightly over)
   └─ Perfect = 1.0, Under = <1.0, Over = >1.0

⚙️ PREDICTION LOGIC FOR EACH CONDITION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[OBESITY]
  • Primary Factor: BMI
  • Logic: High BMI → High obesity risk
  • Threshold: BMI > 28 (normalized 0.7)

[DIABETES]
  • Primary Factors: BMI + Diet Quality
  • Logic: High BMI AND Poor Diet → High diabetes risk
  • Thresholds: BMI > 24 AND diet_score < 0.4

[HEART DISEASE]
  • Primary Factors: BMI + Activity Level
  • Logic: High BMI AND Low Activity → High heart risk
  • Thresholds: BMI > 24 AND activity < 0.3

[NUTRIENT DEFICIENCY]
  • Primary Factor: Diet Quality
  • Logic: Poor Diet → High deficiency risk
  • Threshold: diet_score < 0.3
